About Clayville Gardens

Our history, mission, and vision for the community

Our Mission

Clayville Gardens SDA Church exists to glorify God by proclaiming the everlasting gospel, nurturing believers in their faith journey, and serving our community with Christ's love. We are committed to preparing hearts for the soon return of Jesus Christ.

Our Vision

To be a vibrant, welcoming community of believers who actively share God's love through worship, discipleship, and service, making a lasting impact in Clayville and beyond.

Our History

Clayville Gardens SDA Church has been a beacon of hope and faith in our community for over a decade. From humble beginnings in a small fellowship hall, we have grown into a diverse, multigenerational congregation committed to serving God and our neighbors. Through the years, we have remained steadfast in our mission to share the gospel and prepare people for Christ's return.

Our Values

Biblical Truth

Grounded in Scripture and Seventh-day Adventist beliefs

Inclusive Community

Welcoming all who seek God, regardless of background

Compassionate Service

Meeting needs with Christ's love in our community

Spiritual Growth

Nurturing faith through discipleship and mentorship

Mission Focus

Sharing the gospel locally and globally
