Service Times & Programs

Join us for worship, prayer, and fellowship throughout the sabbath

Morning Manna & Prayer Band

Saturdays • 8:30 – 9:00 AM

This is the spiritual start of the Sabbath. Members gather for short devotional reading, prayers, and personal reflection. It helps everyone focus on God and prepares hearts for the day’s worship.

Song Service

Saturdays • 9:00 – 9:30 AM

Congregational singing and worship. Lifts spirits, unites members in praise, and encourages active participation in worship.

Sabbath School

Saturdays • 9:30 – 10:45 PM

This combines the study of the weekly Sabbath School Lesson and discussion. Members reflect on scripture, ask questions, and share insights.

Divine Service

Saturdays • 11:00 – 12:30 PM

The main worship service of the Sabbath. Includes opening prayers, scripture readings, sermons, special presentations, and tithes and offerings. This is the core teaching and worship session.

Lunch - Fellowship

Saturdays • 12:30 – 14:30 PM

Time to rest, enjoy meals together, and engage in fellowship. There is always free and DELICIOUS lunch served.

Afternoon Service

Saturdays • 14:30 – 16:30 PM

Extended worship, Bible teachings, or special programs. Can include evangelistic meetings, workshops, or ministry updates. Provides spiritual refreshment and a conclusion to the Sabbath activities.
